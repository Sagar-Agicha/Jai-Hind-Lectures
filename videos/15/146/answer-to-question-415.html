Dear User,

LibreOffice Base is the database front-end of the LibreOffice suite.
Base is the equivalent of Microsoft Access. Base is free and open source software, free of cost and free to use and distribute.

With Base, you can store organized data, perform data entries and view data using forms, retrieve information using queries and design and generate nice, printer-ready reports.
