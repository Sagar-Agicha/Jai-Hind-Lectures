You can create a free trial site on -
- https://www.gnomio.com/
- https://moodlecloud.com/

Though there are a few limitations like you cannot install any additional plugins etc.
But, for a start, you can always use these options for practising and getting a hands-on experience.
You can practice from 'Tutorial 4 - Admin dashboard' onwards.

And when you are comfortable using the UI, you can go back to the initial videos and try installing it in your local machine.
Then, you can also go ahead and initiate the process of having a Moodle system for your college by either setting it up on your college server or creating it on one of the two options mentioned above.
